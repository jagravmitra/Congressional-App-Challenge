Who Are We?

Print.Code.Revolutionis a website made so that random people can create toy designs and earn money by 3D printing them and sending them to kids through our company.

With this website people can actually make use of their 3D printers.

How Can You Create Designs?

Designs can be created through our designers page. You will have to signup through our designers signup.

Don't have a printer? We can print for you or have other print it.
